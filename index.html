About SVCE


Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE) is an engineering institute in Tamil Nadu, at Pennalur, Sriperumbudur near Chennai.
SVCE was found in the year 1985. The college was established by the Southern Petrochemical Industries Corporation (SPIC) group. SVCE is among the top engineering colleges of Anna University in Tamil Nadu and a Tier-I institution among self-financing colleges.

  2. Administrative staff :    210
  3. Students :                      3500
  4. Undergraduates :          3260
  5. Postgraduates :          340
  6. Location :    Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
  7. Campus :    Suburban, 95 acres (0.4 km2)
